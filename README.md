 Gully Score

A modern cricket scoring and tournament management platform designed for local cricket communities, tournaments, academies, and street cricket matches.

 About The Project

Gully Score is a web-based cricket management platform that simplifies match scoring, tournament organization, team management, and player statistics tracking.

The goal of the project is to provide local cricket players and organizers with a professional digital scoring experience that is easy to use on both desktop and mobile devices.

 Problem Solved

Many local cricket matches still rely on paper scorebooks and manual record keeping. This makes player statistics, tournament tracking, and match history difficult to manage.

Gully Score provides a centralized digital platform where organizers, players, and teams can manage cricket activities efficiently.
